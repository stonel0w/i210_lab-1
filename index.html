Hometown: Fishers, IN

Location

Fishers is located about 30 minutes northeast of Indianapolis. It's neighboring cities are Westfield, Noblesville, and Carmel. Fishers is in the southeast section of Hamilton County, alongside the west side of the White River. Near the southeastern part of the city, (where i grew up), lies the Geist Reservoir, which covers three counties and contains about 6,100,000,000 gallons of water.
